Things I Enjoy

Hobbies, sports, and more!

Endurance Running

I’ve been running for about seven years now and I truly love it! It’s such a pure sport with a low barrier of entry, and as such has a great community everywhere I go. Running teaches a lesson with every step and is a good escape from the desk and my worries.

Most recently, I was training for my first marathon in March, hoping to qualify for the Boston Marathon. Although those plans were instantly dissolved by the pandemic, I still run weekly. Hopefully 2021 will be kinder!

The Outdoors

There’s just something so relaxing about going outside, especially when it comes with awesome views! Hiking someplace and taking in nature along the way sometimes is a great “detox” from the city. Although my recent adventuring has been somewhat limited because of classes, not having a car, and the pandemic, I always look forward to getting outside somewhere new!

This photo is from a hike with the Northeastern University Hus-Skiers and Outing Club in the White Mountains. It was a foggy and somewhat dreary day, but a beautiful hike nonetheless.

Music

Playing the piano has been a long-time hobby of mine! I recently picked it up again after a year-long hiatus because college got too busy. I’m still rusty and relearning songs, but am enjoying every minute of practice. It’s calming yet rewarding, which is a perfect and elusive combo.

I also love listening to music ranging across several genres. One of my favorite artists I’ve found lately is ANIMA!, and the most controversial is 100 Gecs (for the record, I think they’re pretty cool).
